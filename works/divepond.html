Fishpond Diving Sustainable Art Festival

To promote arts and conservation, Art Together Limited and Hong Kong Bird Watching Society organized the first art festival at the fishpond in Hong Kong. In 2019, Fishpond Diving Sustainable Art Festival was set with an aim on participatory artwork with a series of art education workshops conducted on site. The scale of the festival was larger than the previous year.
