Fishpond Sustainable Art Festival

Fishpond Sustainable Art Festival, organzied by Art Together Limited and Hong Kong Bird Watching Society, invited local artists to bring nature into their artworks and introduce sustainability and fishpond through various creative mediums. Activities covering art exhibtion, workshop, screening, talk and eco tour by local artists were presented in the fishpond of Tai Sai Wan, Yuen Long.


Year: 2018


Roles:

  • Project Management
  • Exhibition Setup
  • Visual Identity Design
